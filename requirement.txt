buat aplikasi pokmeon yang datanya diambil dari pokeAPI
fungsionaitas yang di mau :
- (mandatory) nampilin list pokemon
- (mandatory) ada navaigasi
- (mandatory) ada pagination
- (mandatory) halaman detail statistik pokemon
- (optional) kalau bisa ada game catch atau run lebih bagus 
- (optional) simpan pokemon yang di catch di local storage
- (optional) bisa simpan nama pokemon yang disimpan di local storage
- (optional) bisa hapus pokemon
- (optional) bisa lightmode/darkmode


=== API ===
list pokemon: https://pokeapi.co/api/v2/pokemon?offset=0&limit=20"
image: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PokeAPI/sprites/master/sprites/pokemon/100.png
gif:  https://raw.githubusercontent.com/PokeAPI/sprites/master/sprites/pokemon/other/showdown/1.gif
pokemon habitat: https://pokeapi.co/api/v2/pokemon-habitat/id
pokemon habitat from species : https://pokeapi.co/api/v2/pokemon-species/1
